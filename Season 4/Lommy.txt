= Two years later, Arya sees Polliver at a tavern. She explains to [[Sandor Clegane|the Hound]] who Polliver was and that he had killed Lommy, to which the Hound responds in deadpan bemusement, "What the fuck's a Lommy?". Determined to pay Polliver back for Lommy's death, Arya cripples him while reciting the exact words he said to Lommy before killing him; then she stabs him through the neck with Needle, thus avenging Lommy."[[Two Swords]]"
