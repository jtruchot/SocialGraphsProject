 2]]=== Queen Selyse is present at the ritual burning of the statues of [[Faith of the Seven|the Seven]] on the shores of [[Dragonstone (island)|Dragonstone]], standing at the side of her husband, the King. When Stannis pulls the flaming [[Lightbringer]] from one of the statues, she kneels alongside the other worshipers of the [[R'hllor|Lord of Light]]. After the ceremony is over, Stannis extends his hand to her and Selyse rises to her feet to walk after him."[[The North Remembers]]"  Melisandre notes that Selyse has failed to give Stannis a son, but only stillborn boys, and that Selyse is sickly and often confined to her tower at Dragonstone."[[The Night Lands]]"  Stannis later reminds Ser [[Davos Seaworth]] how his wife Selyse nearly died during the [[Siege of Storm's End|siege]] of [[Storm's End]] during [[Robert's Rebellion]] before Davos was able to breach the lines and bring in supplies."[[The Prince of Winterfell]]"  ===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|
