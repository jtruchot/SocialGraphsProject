= Drennan is a crewman of the ''Sea Bitch'', the ship [[Theon Greyjoy|Theon]]'s father [[Balon Greyjoy]] gave him to raid the [[Stony Shore]]. He takes part in the [[Fall of Winterfell|seizing]] of [[Winterfell]]. When the people of the castle are brought together in the courtyard for [[Bran Stark|Bran]] to officially surrender to Theon, [[Farlen]] yells insults at Theon, and Drennan hits him with a club."[[The Old Gods and the New]]"  [[left|Osha kills Drennan.]] Afterwards, Drennan is assigned guard duty. During the night, Osha seduces him and slits his throat, allowing Bran and [[Rickon Stark|Rickon]] to escape Winterfell alongside [[Hodor]], [[Summer]] and [[Shaggydog]] and Osha herself.  [[Theon beats [[Black Lorren]] next to Drennan's dead body.]] Drennan's body is discovered and brought into the courtyard the following day."[[A Man Without Honor]]"
