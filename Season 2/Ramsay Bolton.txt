 2]]=== After [[Winterfell]] is [[Fall of Winterfell|seized]] by [[Theon Greyjoy]] for the [[Iron Islands|ironborn]], Ramsay is dispatched by his father [[Roose Bolton]] to re-take it in the name of King [[Robb Stark]]. Ramsay's forces besiege the castle and he infuriates Theon by blowing a horn all through the night. The following morning, Theon rallies his men to defend the castle, but he is betrayed and knocked out cold by his men, who accept Robb Stark's offer of mercy for the [[ironborn]] garrison if they surrender and hand over Theon.
