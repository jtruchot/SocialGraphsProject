 5]]=== Vala is approached by [[White Rat]], an [[Unsullied]], who has been repeatedly frequenting her. White Rat pays her, then stops her from disrobing and removing his trousers - as a [[Eunuchs|eunuch]] he is unable to have intercourse - instead having her lie behind him and caress him as she hums a melody to him. However, she is in league with the Sons of the Harpy and, as he is distracted, one of them enters the room and slits his throat.
