= Eventually, Littlefinger is forced to answer for his many crimes, including his role in the [[Assassination of Jon Arryn]], when [[Sansa Stark|Sansa]] unexpectedly puts him on [[Trial of Petyr Baelish|trial]]. Sansa publicly accuses Littlefinger of persuading Lysa (whom Littlefinger also murdered) to poison Jon and write a letter to her sister, falsely accusing the Lannisters of killing her husband, igniting the conflict between the two great houses. Sansa has [[Arya Stark|Arya]] slit his throat, exacting justice for Jon."[[The Dragon and the Wolf]]"
