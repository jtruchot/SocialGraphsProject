 1]]=== [[Loras Tyrell|Loras]] tells [[Renly Baratheon|Renly]] that his father would support Renly's claim to the [[Iron Throne]]. Renly rejects the notion, pointing out he is fourth in line to the throne, but Loras explains that he could fight for Renly and his father would bankroll their armies. Renly seems more intrigued by the notion"[[The Wolf and the Lion]]" and later accepts the plan, though he fails to win the support of Eddard Stark."[[You Win or You Die]]" Along with Loras, Renly flees the city of [[King's Landing]] and heads south towards the Tyrell homelands.
