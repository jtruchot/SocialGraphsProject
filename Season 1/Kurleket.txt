= Kurleket is drinking with companions at the [[Inn at the Crossroads]] when [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]] arrives on his way south. [[Catelyn Stark|Catelyn]] is also in the inn. She turns to the knights from the [[Riverlands]] to help her to arrest Tyrion, accusing him of attempting to murder [[Bran Stark|Bran]]. She asserts her origin as a daughter of [[House Tully]], and then tells Kurleket that she recognizes the red stallion of [[House Bracken]] and says that her father, Lord [[Hoster Tully]] of [[Riverrun]], considers Lord [[Jonos Bracken]] one of his most loyal bannermen. Kurleket draws his sword and threatens Tyrion."[[Cripples, Bastards, and Broken Things]]"  [[Kurleket holds his sword on Tyrion Lannister at the Crossroads Inn.]] Kurleket is among the men who accompany Catelyn into the Vale. The party is attacked by warriors from the [[hill tribes]], and Kurleket dies defending Catelyn."[[The Wolf and the Lion]]"
