= Stiv, Osha, and Wallen flee south of the Wall, apparently in fear of the return of the [[White Walkers]]. In the woods near [[Winterfell]] they stumble across [[Bran Stark|Bran]] and decide to steal his horse. Realizing his importance, they ponder taking him prisoner and returning him to [[Mance Rayder]], the king-beyond-the-wall, for a reward, but decide against it. [[Robb Stark|Robb]] intervenes, kills Wallen and disables Osha. Stiv is killed by [[Theon Greyjoy|Theon]]."[[A Golden Crown]]"
