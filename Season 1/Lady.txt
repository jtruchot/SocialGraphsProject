= Lady was adopted as a pup with the rest of the Stark direwolves, when [[Eddard Stark]] and his entourage came upon the pups and their deceased mother. At [[Jon Snow]]'s request, the pups were spared and each Stark child adopted one as their own. Sansa named her direwolf Lady."[[Winter Is Coming]]"  Having grown to the size of an adult dog already, Lady accompanies Sansa when she moves to [[King's Landing]]. While they are near the [[Inn at the Crossroads]], however, she is executed by her father after Queen [[Cersei Lannister|Cersei]] spitefully demands she be killed in place of her sister [[Nymeria (direwolf)|Nymeria]], who was driven away by [[Arya Stark|Arya]] to save her from certain death after biting [[Joffrey Baratheon|Joffrey]] in defense of Arya. Although [[Ilyn Payne]] was supposed to kill Lady, Ned carried out the sentence himself citing that, being of the [[North]], she deserves better than a butcher. At the moment she is killed, [[Bran Stark|Bran]] awakes from his coma."[[The Kingsroad]]"
