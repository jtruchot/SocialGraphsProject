 1]]=== [[Rodrik presides over Will's execution.]] Ser Rodrik informs Lord [[Eddard Stark]] that [[Will]], a deserter from the [[Night's Watch]], has been caught in the hills. He accompanies Eddard to Will's execution. On the way back to Winterfell he witnesses the discovery of the six [[direwolf]] pups in the Wolfswood. The Stark children adopt the pups. King [[Robert Baratheon]] visits Winterfell and Ser Rodrik assembles with the rest of the household to welcome him. Robert asks Eddard to serve as [[hand of the King]]. [[Bran Stark]] is found severely injured at the base of a disused tower."[[Winter Is Coming]]"  Eddard goes south to [[King's Landing]] and [[Jory Cassel|Jory]] accompanies him as the Captain of his personal guard while Rodrik remains in Winterfell. [[Catspaw assassin|An assassin]] infiltrates the castle and attempts to kill Bran but is foiled by [[Catelyn Stark|Catelyn]] and Bran's direwolf [[Summer]]. Rodrik identifies the blade carried by the assassin as [[Valyrian steel]]. Catelyn suspects [[House Lannister]] (due to a blonde strand of hair found at the tower) and decides to warn Eddard personally. Rodrik offers to send a squad of guards with her but she is concerned about drawing attention. He offers to accompany her personally and she agrees."[[The Kingsroad]]" [[178x178px|Rodrick and Cat in King's Landing.]] Rodrik and Catelyn arrive in King's Landing. He escorts her during through the city and they are told that [[Petyr Baelish|Littlefinger]] lost the blade used by the assassin gambling with [[Tyrion Lannister]]."[[Lord Snow]]" They set out for Winterfell. At the [[Inn at the Crossroads]], Rodrik unsuccessfully attempts to dismiss the singer [[Marillion]]. He helps Catelyn take [[Tyrion Lannister]] prisoner when he arrives at the inn."[[Cripples, Bastards, and Broken Things]]" As they head east through the Vale of Arryn via the [[Eastern Road]]. Rodrik helps fend off an attack by the hill tribes and receives a minor wound, but is able to complete the journey to the [[Eyrie]]. Rodrik's nephew [[Jory Cassel]] is killed by [[Jaime Lannister]] while protecting Eddard in King's Landing."[[The Wolf and the Lion]]"  Rodrik witnesses Tyrion's [[First trial by combat of Tyrion Lannister|trial by combat]] and is disappointed when Tyrion's [[Bronn|champion]] wins."[[A Golden Crown]]" King Robert dies following a hunting mishap and Eddard questions the lineage of his successor, [[Joffrey Baratheon]]. Eddard is arrested for treason and held in the dungeons of King's Landing"[[You Win or You Die]]" until the newly-crowned Joffrey [[Execution of Eddard Stark|orders him beheaded]], prompting the North to secede and rise in anger and rebellion.  [[Rodrik watches as Robb announces their victory at Whispering Wood.]] Rodrik accompanies Catelyn to [[Moat Cailin]], where they find [[Robb Stark]]'s army camped, where he greets [[Greatjon Umber]] like a longtime friend."[[The Pointy End]]" Robb needs to cross the river [[Trident]] but is unsure of the support of Lord [[Walder Frey]]. Rodrik advises Catelyn to not go into the Twins alone to talk to Lord Frey. She does so anyway and negotiates safe passage.
