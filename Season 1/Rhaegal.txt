 1]]=== [[Rhaegal, the right, green dragon egg.]] Daenerys is given three dragons eggs by Magister [[Illyrio Mopatis]] on the occasion of her wedding to [[Khal]] [[Drogo]]."[[Winter Is Coming]]" She is drawn to these eggs and always keeps them in a chest with her. [[Viserys Targaryen (son of Aerys II)|Viserys]] tries to steal them, but is stopped by Ser [[Jorah Mormont]]."[[A Golden Crown]]"  Rhaegal is born along with his two siblings after Daenerys has their eggs placed on the funeral pyre erected for her deceased husband, Khal Drogo.
