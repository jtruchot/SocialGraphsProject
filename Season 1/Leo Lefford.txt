= Leo Lefford has dinner with [[Tywin Lannister|Tywin]] and his other commanders the night before their victory in the [[Battle of the Green Fork]]."[[Baelor]]" He is present for a strategy meeting with Tywin following the defeat in the [[Battle of the Whispering Wood]]. He wears a blue and yellow cloak over his armor. Also present are [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]], [[Kevan Lannister|Kevan]], [[Addam Marbrand]], another bannerman, Tywin's servant and several Lannister officers. He reports that [[Grey Wind]] killed a dozen men and as many horses during the battle. He believes that they should try to ransom [[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]]. He also suggests returning to [[Casterly Rock]] to raise levies. Tywin dismisses his bannermen and then announces plans to relocate to [[Harrenhal]]."[[Fire and Blood]]"
