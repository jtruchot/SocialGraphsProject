= When Tyrion is urging Jon to [[Assassination of Daenerys Targaryen|assassinate Daenerys]], Jon struggles with the idea due to his love for Daenerys. He reflects on Aemon's wisdom, "Love is the death of duty," telling Tyrion that Maester Aemon told him this long ago. Tyrion reverses Aemon's words to tell Jon, "Sometimes, duty is the death of love.""[[The Iron Throne]]"
