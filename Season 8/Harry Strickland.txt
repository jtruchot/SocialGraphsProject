= Strickland and the Golden Company are brought to [[Westeros]] by [[Euron Greyjoy]]. He oversees a division of the Golden Company on the ''[[Silence]]'', Euron's flagship. He later meets with [[King of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men|Queen]] [[Cersei Lannister|Cersei]] in the [[Great Hall|throne room]] of the [[Red Keep]] alongside Euron. Strickland affirms the Golden Company's contract with Cersei, but reports although that they have brought their 18,000 infantry and 2,000 cavalry, their [[elephant]]s are absent due to their inability to travel via sea voyage, much to Cersei's disappointment. Nonetheless, she welcomes him to [[King's Landing]], after which he expresses placid eagerness to fight for her."[[Winterfell (episode)|Winterfell]]" [[Harry commands the Golden Company during the Battle of King's Landing.]] Strickland leads the Golden Company outside the gates of King's Landing at the beginning of the [[Battle of King's Landing]], facing off against [[Jon Snow]], [[Davos Seaworth|Davos]], [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]], and their armies outside the gates. However, [[Daenerys Targaryen|Daenerys]] arrives with [[Drogon]] and burns through the city's walls and the Golden Company, knocking Strickland off his horse. Strickland attempts to run back into the city, but is skewered through the back by [[Grey Worm]] while the [[House Targaryen|Targaryen]], [[House Stark|Stark]], and [[House Arryn|Arryn]] forces charge into the city."[[The Bells]]"
