One of four women in the world who can perform a proper Meereenese Knot.|Tyrion Lannister|Walk of Punishment (episode) '''Kayla''' is a [[Prostitution|prostitute]] who works in [[Littlefinger's brothel]].  == Biography == [[Image:Kayla3.jpg|thumb|Kayla is a talented contortionist.]] Kayla is a prostitute and a highly skilled contortionist. According to [[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]], she is one of only four women in the [[known world]] who can bend her body to the extent that she can properly perform a sexual position known as the "[[Meereenese Knot]]".  === [[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]] === Along with [[Marei]] and [[Genna]], she is one of the three prostitutes in Littlefinger's brothel who are hired by Tyrion to service [[Podrick Payne|Pod]] as a reward for saving his life in the [[Battle of the Blackwater]]."[[Walk of Punishment (episode)|Walk of Punishment]]" Kayla and her colleagues are apparently so satisfied with Podrick that they decline payment, much to the astonishment of Tyrion, [[Bronn]], [[Ros]], and even [[Varys]]."[[And Now His Watch Is Ended]]"  === [[Game of Thrones: Season 4|''Game of Thrones'': Season 4]] === Kayla is present as an entertainer at the [[Purple Wedding|Joffrey's wedding]].
