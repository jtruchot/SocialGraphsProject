Both were younger sons of [[Kevan Lannister]], and younger brothers of [[Lancel Lannister|Lancel]].  ==Biography== ===[[Game of Thrones: Season 3|''Game of Thrones'': Season 3]]=== Willem and his brother Martyn are captured by the forces of [[House Tully]] led by [[Edmure Tully]] at the [[Battle of Stone Mill]]. The two are kept as [[hostage]]s at [[Riverrun]].  After [[Robb Stark|Robb]] visits Riverrun to attend [[Hoster Tully|his grandfather]]'s funeral, Willem and Martyn are visited in their cell by [[Talisa Stark|Talisa]], who tends to Martyn's wounded hand.
