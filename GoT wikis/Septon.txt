[[File:Septon 2x10.png|150px|right|thumb|An unnamed septon, who marries Robb Stark and Talisa Maegyr.]] Quote|The Septon has prayed his prayers.|Lord [[Walder Frey]]|The Rains of Castamere (episode) '''Septons''' are the male clergy of the [[Faith of the Seven]]. They preside over religious ceremonies such as weddings. They have taken vows of celibacy, and often take important positions in a noble's household, attending the local septs at castles and keeps. In contrast, female clergy are known as [[septa]]s.
