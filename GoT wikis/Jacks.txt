He is a member of the household guard at Winterfell.  Jacks is one of the guards who accompany [[Eddard Stark]] to [[King's Landing]]. He and [[Varly]] escort Eddard when he visits the armorer [[Tobho Mott]] and discovers [[Gendry Baratheon|Gendry]], one of [[Robert Baratheon]]'s bastard.  He is one of the few survivors of the [[Massacre in the Red Keep|massacre of the Stark household]]. He is later sent with [[Alton Lannister#In the books|Cleos Frey]] to the Starks' camp, to deliver [[Tyrion Lannister]]'s counterterms and Eddard's bones. He is then put under the command of [[Hallis Mollen]].  He is sent north with Hallis Mollen's men to escort Eddard's bones back to Winterfell. Before they reach [[Moat Cailin]], however, the [[Ironborn invasion of the North|ironborn invade the North]].
