= Willem and his brother Martyn are captured by the forces of [[House Tully]] led by [[Edmure Tully]] at the [[Battle of Stone Mill]]. The two are kept as [[hostage]]s at [[Riverrun]].  After [[Robb Stark|Robb]] visits Riverrun to attend [[Hoster Tully|his grandfather]]'s funeral, Willem and Martyn are visited in their cell by [[Talisa Stark|Talisa]], who tends to Martyn's wounded hand. Willem looks on in silence while Martyn asks if the tales told among the Lannisters about Robb turning into a wolf at night or devouring his enemies are true."[[Walk of Punishment (episode)|Walk of Punishment]]" Earlier, Robb criticizes Edmure for the Battle of the Stone Mill and concludes that Willem and Martyn are not valuable enough to exchange for [[Sansa Stark|his]] [[Arya Stark|sisters]].  Martyn and Willem are later murdered inside their cells by [[Rickard Karstark]] and five other men, in retribution for the death of his [[Torrhen Karstark|son]] by [[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]]."[[Kissed by Fire]]"
